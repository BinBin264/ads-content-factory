# Demo Case: Coin Scanner App

Use this as the first end-to-end demo scenario.

```text
Product name:
Coin Scanner App

Category:
Mobile app

Description:
An app that helps users scan old coins, identify coin details, and view estimated reference value.

Audience:
People who find old coins at home, casual collectors, adults with coin jars.

Goal:
app_install

Platform:
TikTok

Duration:
20s

Tone:
Natural UGC, relatable, curiosity-driven

CTA:
Download now and scan your old coins

Claims to avoid:
Guaranteed value, 100% accurate appraisal, you will make money
```

Expected MVP hooks:

- Variant A: "I almost spent this coin..."
- Variant B: "Here's how to check an old coin in 5 seconds."
